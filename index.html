Plot a route using the in-game galaxy map, and then import the route here to have it adjusted for a fleet carrier.

Using a ship with a smaller jump range will hit more stars along the route, which will give your carrier route more efficient jump options.

Other route calculation programs rely on star data collected by players, while this lets you take advantage of the fact that the in-game route planner knows about many stars not in any player-built star database.
